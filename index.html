About

I'm a writer and a creative soul. My work moves between fiction, poetry, and the spoken word stage, and keeps circling the same questions: what holds people together, what helps them heal, and where beauty hides in ordinary days.

My stories live on Wattpad and Weink, my reflections land in Ewuraesi Ink Impressions on Substack, and my pieces have appeared in publications like Unspoken Words Magazine and The Creatives Project GH. Through GhanaReads, I'm working to get more young Ghanaians falling in love with reading.
